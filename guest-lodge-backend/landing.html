Questions

Do I need to be technical?
No. You send us room photos, rates, and your hotel name. We build everything. Your front desk app is just a webpage on their phone — no app store, no downloads.
How do guests find my booking site?
We add your direct booking link to your Google Business Profile. When someone finds you on Google Maps or Search, they click "Book" and go to your site instead of Booking.com. Same traffic you're already getting — just routed to you directly.
Will this mess up my current operations?
No. A booking comes in, your front desk gets a notification with the guest's name, phone, room, and dates. They handle it the same way they handle a phone reservation. Nothing changes about your check-in process.
Should I remove my OTA listings?
No — keep them. This gives you a direct channel alongside your OTAs. Over time, more guests book direct (0% commission) instead of through Booking.com (15-20%). You gradually shift revenue without losing any bookings.
Do guests really pay $0 at booking?
Yes. We place a $1 card verification (released instantly) to prevent fake bookings. The guest pays the full amount at your front desk when they check in. This increases conversion dramatically — people are 3x more likely to book when there's no upfront charge.
What happens after 12 months?
Hosting continues at $97/month. Or you can self-host — we hand over everything. No lock-in ever.
What's the money-back guarantee?
If you're not live and happy within 7 days, full refund. No questions.
Can I run Facebook ads to my booking site?
Absolutely — it's built for that. The checkout is optimized for cold traffic from social media ads. Handles in-app browsers, slow connections, and impatient scrollers. Refined over months of real ad spend.
